About Us


We are two college students that enjoy using our creativity to create delicious treats. We originally just made chocolate covered strawberries to raise money but soon enough we found out we really enjoyed making them and coming up with new ideas. We also had several people that loved our strawberries and wanted us to create more designs for them. Since then we have had a lot of fun trying out different combinations of toppings, messages, colors, and designs. We are thankful for all the support we have received and hope to continue to create chocolate covered strawberries for everyone in our community! -Margarita & Karla
